6th Circuit Court of Appeals (OH, KY, MI, TN)

Salter v. City of Detroit (2025)

Topic: Brady Violations / Officer Liability

Holding: Affirmed that officers have a clearly established duty to disclose material exculpatory evidence. Failure to do so eliminates qualified immunity.

Novak v. City of Parma (2024)

Topic: Free Speech / Parody

Holding: Arresting a suspect solely for creating a parody police Facebook page without probable cause of a true crime violates the First Amendment.

Mercer v. Athens County (2023)

Topic: Excessive Force / Taser

Holding: Using a Taser on a non-resisting or passively resisting suspect is a violation of clearly established law.
