Topic: 2026 Mandatory CPT Requirements

The Ohio Attorney General has officially released the 2026 Continuing Professional Training (CPT) mandates. All sworn officers must complete 24 hours of training this year. The 8 mandatory hours focus on "Critical Decision-Making," "Medical Aid," and "Tactical Communication."

Legal Updates: Case Law & Legislation

Case/Bill: House Bill 44 (Lateral Hires)

Summary: Signed into law late 2025, HB 44 is now effective. It allows police departments to bypass the civil service exam for applicants with "exceptional qualifications" or prior certification.

Impact: This streamlines hiring for lateral transfers. Departments should update their hiring policies to reflect this new exemption.

Topic: Violent Crime Reduction Grant (FY 2026)

Funding for the 2026 cycle is now active (Jan 1 - Dec 31). The grant focuses on technology for ballistics processing and overtime for investigators in high-crime zones. Agencies should review reporting requirements immediately.
