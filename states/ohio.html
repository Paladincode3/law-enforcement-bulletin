Ohio Legislative & HR Updates

Marijuana & Probable Cause (Issue 2)

With the legalization of recreational marijuana, the "Plain Smell" doctrine is evolving. The smell of raw or burnt marijuana alone may no longer constitute probable cause for a vehicle search in Ohio without other indicators of impairment or illegal distribution.

HB 230: Fentanyl Trafficking

Legislation increasing penalties for drug trafficking involving fentanyl. Creates new felony classifications for volume dealers and enhances sentencing for deaths caused by overdose.

Distracted Driving (SB 288)

Ohio's new hands-free law is now in full effect with primary enforcement. Officers can pull over drivers solely for holding a phone while driving.

OPOTA Certification Updates

New mandates for Continuing Professional Training (CPT) hours for 2026, with a focus on mental health response and de-escalation tactics.
