Officer Safety: Vehicle Search (Odor)

OCT 03, 2025 State v. Gray, 2025-Ohio-4479

Holding: Major Change. The Ohio First District Court of Appeals has ruled that the smell of marijuana alone is no longer sufficient to establish probable cause to search a vehicle. Officers must articulate additional indicators of criminal activity.

Legislation: Lateral Hiring

FEB 04, 2026 House Bill 44

Topic: Civil Service Exemption

Summary: HB 44 is now effective. It authorizes police departments to fill vacant positions without a competitive examination if the applicant possesses a valid OPOTA certificate. This streamlines hiring for lateral transfers.

View Bill Text

Case Law: Extending Traffic Stops

JAN 2025 State v. Dunlap, 2025-Ohio-2323

Topic: Scope of Detention

Impact: The Ohio Supreme Court ruled that officers may extend a traffic stop to verify a driver's license status, even if the driver does not match the description of the registered owner.
