Pennsylvania Legislative & HR Updates

Act 57: Background Checks

Strict requirements for law enforcement agencies to consult the Misconduct Database before hiring. Requires agencies to report separation records for officers who resign while under investigation.

Clean Slate 3.0

New automated sealing of certain drug felony records after 10 years without conviction. Officers conducting background checks must be aware of what data is legally accessible via JNET.

Act 59: PTSD & Use of Force

Mandates mental health evaluations for officers involved in lethal use of force incidents before returning to duty. Also establishes new PTSD presumption for workers' compensation.
