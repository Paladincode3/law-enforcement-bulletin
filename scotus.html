Supreme Court Decisions (2024-2025 Term)

Current analysis of recent U.S. Supreme Court opinions impacting law enforcement procedures, qualified immunity, and criminal procedure.

October Term 2024 (Released 2025)
Barnes v. Felix
Docket No. 23-1239

Topic: Use of Force / "Moment of Threat"
Addressed whether the Fourth Amendment analysis of a police officer's use of deadly force is limited solely to the moment the force was used, or if the officer's pre-seizure conduct can be considered.

Ames v. Ohio Dept. of Youth Services
Docket No. 23-1039

Topic: Title VII Discrimination
Clarified the burden of proof for majority-group plaintiffs (reverse discrimination) in Title VII employment discrimination claims.

Read Opinion (PDF)
Garland v. VanDerStok
Docket No. 23-852

Topic: Firearms / "Ghost Guns"
Examined the ATF's authority to regulate firearm parts kits and "ghost guns" under the Gun Control Act of 1968.

City of San Francisco v. EPA
Docket No. 23-753

Topic: Environmental Regulations
Addressed the specificity required in National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) permits under the Clean Water Act.
