2025 Decisions

Barnes v. Felix

Use of Force / "Moment of Threat" Doctrine
No. 23-1239 DECIDED: MAY 15, 2025
Holding: The Supreme Court unanimously struck down the "Moment of Threat" doctrine. Courts must now consider the "totality of circumstances" leading up to a use of force incident, not just the split-second before the trigger was pulled.

Ames v. Ohio Dept of Youth Services

Employment / Reverse Discrimination
No. 23-1039 DECIDED: JUNE 5, 2025
Holding: The Court ruled that majority-group plaintiffs (e.g., straight/white employees) do not need to meet a higher "background circumstances" burden to prove discrimination under Title VII. The standard is now equal for all.
