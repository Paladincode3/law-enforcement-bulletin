Salter v. City of Detroit (6th Cir. 2025) Topic: Brady Violations / Qualified Immunity

Affirmed that officers have a clearly established duty to disclose material exculpatory evidence. Failure to do so eliminates qualified immunity protection.

Cunningham v. Shelby County (6th Cir. 2024) Topic: Deliberate Indifference / Jail Medical Care

Addressed the standard for "deliberate indifference" regarding medical care for detainees, emphasizing that reckless disregard is sufficient for liability.
